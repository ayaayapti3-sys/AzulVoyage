WHEN TO GO

There is no wrong season
to discover Morocco

In spring, we recommend the foothills of the Atlas Mountains and the Ourika Valley, when everything is in bloom.

In summer, enjoy the refreshing breeze of Essaouira and cool hikes in the High Atlas Mountains.

In autumn, head to the Sahara and Morocco's imperial cities, where mild days and golden light create the perfect atmosphere.

In winter, Marrakech, the Sahara and southern Morocco offer a warm, peaceful and pleasant escape.
